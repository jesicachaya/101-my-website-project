A little bit about me.

I am a first generation Lebanese American and was born and raised in New England. My journey into tech is quite the zig-zag of a path! I had my first taste in a coding class in college as I was double majoring in math and education. I really enjoyed the challenge of problem-solving and creating something meaningful from scratch and that stuck with me!

That sparked something behind a door that could never be shut. But first I dipped my toes into a few different careers - high school math teacher, personal trainer, health coach for a startup app, amongst other things. Weird path? Maybe. But wouldn't change it for anything because my past experiences allow me to enter tech with so much valuable fieldwork under my belt that allow me a unique perspective on the variety of projects I get to work on! When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code. Just as I've chased my passion, I'd love to help you chase yours!
